## Daily Practice

<div class="daily-practice" markdown="1">
**Morning** &mdash; 10 minutes of silence. Ask: *"Where can I reduce suffering today?"*

**Midday** &mdash; One honest check-in with another person.

**Evening** &mdash; Review actions. Acknowledge harm. Plan repair.

**Night** &mdash; Release comparison. Bless all beings. Rest without digital noise.
</div>

*From [Book XII: Mindfulness &amp; Practice](/docs/en/book-vi.html)*
